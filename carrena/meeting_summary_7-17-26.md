# Topics Discussed

## 1. PDF Court Forms Automation (Initially Discussed)

### Background

The company previously had a system that automatically filled eviction court forms.

In 2025, the courts updated all official forms, making the old templates obsolete.

The original idea was to recreate the automation using the new court-issued PDF forms.

### Current Workflow

- Staff enters client information once.
- Information automatically populates every page of the eviction package.
- Case number, tenant information, address, dates, and court information remain synchronized across all forms.
- Depending on the stage of the eviction process, they print only the pages required.

### Discussion

As the conversation progressed, it became clear that this workflow requires a deep understanding of eviction procedures and legal documentation.

Christopher has already built similar automation in the past and fully understands their internal process.

### Outcome

The client decided that this project will likely remain in-house.

**Do not include this service in the proposal.**
