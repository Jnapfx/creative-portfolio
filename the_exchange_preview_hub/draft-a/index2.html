About the pilot

A simple, guided way to explore real-world connections.

The Exchange is being developed as a pilot to help employers, students, early-career professionals, and community partners connect around workplace learning, career exposure, and project-based experiences. During the pilot phase, submissions are reviewed by the program team before any follow-up or introduction takes place.
